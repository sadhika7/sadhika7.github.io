My Projects

Some of my selective projects.

Social networking site for students


"Learn" is a social networking website for students where there are two kinds of users: admin and students. The admin adds and assigns the students to their respective classes and the students can then join their classes and have discussions with their fellow classmates on the discussion page. The students can like and comment on each other's posts and can also directly message fellow classmates.The website is built with HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, MySQL AND AJAX.


A food delivery website


"Food Mood" is an academic group project which is a food order and delivery website. There are three kinds of users: restaurant owner, customer and driver. The restaurant owner can add his restaurants and its food menu to the website and receive orders. The customers can order food from restaurants that have registered on Food Mood and the driver can register for delivering food to the restaurant's customers. The website is built using HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, JavaScript and Firebase.


Thesis on sockpuppet detection


This is the thesis for 'Sally McDonnell Barksdale Honors College' for my Bachelors degree from the University of Mississippi. Under my thesis advisor Philip Rhodes, I created a sockpuppet (fake accounts) detection tool using Python, pySpark, Hadoop and the machine learning algorithms: k-means clustering and multi-layer perceptron classifier.


A travel and tours website


Natours is a website for travel and tours where you can view various tour packages and book for them. I built this site as a part of a Udemy course by Jonas.io and have added certain elements and features to the site he initially built. The site is built with HTML5, CSS, Node.js, MongoDB, Express.js and is deployed on Heroku.
